Successfully designed and built an up-scaled experimental setup to investigate surface gravity and capillary waves. 

The work included the integration of a high-speed camera system with a custom-built Arduino synchronization circuit for a marble dropper, ensuring millisecond-precision in data acquisition. Various optical noise reduction techniques were applied to enhance the fidelity of the results.

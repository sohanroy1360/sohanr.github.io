I am thrilled to share that I have successfully defended my Master’s thesis! 

The research was conducted at **GKN Aerospace**, Sweden, where I focused on physics-driven optimization of intermediate compressor duct. This experience allowed me to apply inhouse CFD solvers along with metaheuristic optimizers to real-world industrial challenges. I am grateful to my mentors at both KTH, Dr. Jens Fransson and GKN, Dr. Jonathan Bergh and Blanka Tonsic, for their invaluable guidance throughout this journey.
